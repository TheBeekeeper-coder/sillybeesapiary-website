Life lessons from watching the bees.

We've learned a lot from the hive.

The queen is cared for and provided for ‚Äî not because she demands it, but because the hive knows her role matters. There's no resentment. Just provision.

And when the hive grows too full, they don't fight for space. They plan. They raise a new queen. They split ‚Äî one family becoming two, both stronger for it.

Work. Service. Provision. Growth.

The hive has a lot to teach.
